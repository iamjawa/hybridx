# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Independent plant breeders — professionals and serious hobbyists managing breeding programmes for ornamentals (roses, perennials), crops, or specialty plants. They work on multiple generations simultaneously, track thousands of seedlings per season, and need precision in pedigree, trait evaluation, and selection decisions. They use laptops in the field and at their desk; mobile for quick checks and QR label scanning.

## Product Purpose

HybridX is the operating system for plant breeders. It replaces spreadsheets, paper notebooks, and fragmented tools with one workspace to track pedigrees, plan crosses, evaluate seedlings, and manage the full breeding lifecycle — from cross to cultivar. Success means a breeder can spend more time looking at plants and less time looking at their screen.

## Brand Personality

Calm, premium, trustworthy, fast, obvious. Like a well-bred plant: every part is intentional, nothing is excessive, and the result is quietly exceptional. The brand speaks with the confidence of expertise — no hype, no decoration, just clear purpose.

## Anti-references

- Enterprise software (SAP, Oracle, Salesforce) — heavy, bureaucratic, slow
- CRUD admin panels — grid-of-data with no purpose, no hierarchy, no story
- Windows desktop software — cluttered, inconsistent, no typography
- "AI-generated" design — gradient text, glassmorphism, hero metrics, numbered sections, tiny uppercase eyebrows
- Old plant breeding software — outdated interfaces, poor UX
- Notion-level density in tool areas (good for docs, bad for focused tasks)

## Design Principles

1. **Calm confidence** — The interface never shouts. Every element earns its place. White space is a feature, not waste.
2. **Disappear into the task** — A breeder's focus is the plant, not the tool. Workflows are obvious, friction is removed, and the UI gets out of the way.
3. **Consistency is trust** — One delete dialog, one button style, one form pattern. Familiarity across every screen builds confidence.
4. **Surface what matters** — Dashboard answers "what needs attention", not "here's a chart of everything." Detail pages give immediate context and action.
5. **Premium feel, not enterprise weight** — Inspired by Linear, Arc, and Apple: tight typography, purposeful motion, generous spacing, every pixel intentional.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum (4.5:1 body text, 3:1 large text)
- Keyboard-navigable all workflows
- Screen reader support with ARIA labels and semantic HTML
- Respects `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Minimum 44×44px touch targets on mobile
- Focus-visible indicators on all interactive elements
